A final project for BCITs Technical Web Design program. Over a 6 week period I work along side a team to design and develop a website for Adele Arseneau. As a visual artist she asked for a portfolio site that would showcase her work and was easy enough for her to keep updated. We went for a clean, minimal design and through Wordpress created custom functionality for her to add or remove content as she wished.
